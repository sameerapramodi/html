Transforming Fish Quality Assessment with AI Precision for Yellowfin Tuna

MALUWA application is developed by GeoEdge with generous support from Norway for the Department of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources.

The advanced scanning technology to evaluate various aspects of fish quality, including eyes, skin, and flesh. The app features a centralized database that consolidates scanning results, location data, and quality metrics, ensuring a comprehensive approach to monitoring and managing fish quality.

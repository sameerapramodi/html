Transforming Fish Quality Assessment with AI Precision for Yellowfin Tuna

The "AI-MALUWA" App utilizes advanced scanning technology to evaluate key aspects of fish quality, including the eyes, skin, flesh and gills. Furthermore, a centralized database consolidates scanning results, location data, and quality metrics, providing a comprehensive approach to monitoring and managing fish quality. This ensures transparency and efficiency throughout the entire supply chain.

About Us

Designing Solutions
to Enhance
Seafood Quality

The "AI-Enabled Mobile App for Fish Quality Measurement in Sri Lanka" marks a significant advancement in the seafood industry. Funded by Norway and coordinated by the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO), with support from the Department of Fisheries & Aquatic Resources, this initiative was developed by GeoEDGE (Pvt) Ltd. Its core aim is to empower multiday boat owners, skippers, and fish exporters by enabling them to assess fish quality using AI and machine learning technologies.
